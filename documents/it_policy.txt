IT Policy - ABC Technologies

Password Policy:
Employees must use strong passwords containing uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Passwords should be changed every 90 days.

Email Usage Policy:
Company email accounts must be used only for business-related communication. Sharing confidential information with unauthorized individuals is prohibited.

Internet Usage Policy:
Employees may use the internet for work-related purposes. Accessing unauthorized or harmful websites is not allowed.

Device Usage Policy:
Company-issued laptops and devices should be used responsibly and protected from unauthorized access.

Data Security:
Employees must protect company data and follow security guidelines. Sensitive information should not be shared outside the organization without authorization.

Software Installation:
Only approved software may be installed on company devices. Employees must obtain permission from the IT department before installing new applications.

Remote Access:
Employees working remotely must use secure connections and company-approved VPN services.

Incident Reporting:
Any suspected security breach, phishing attempt, or malware infection should be reported immediately to the IT support team.

Backup Policy:
Important company data is backed up regularly. Employees should store work files in approved company storage systems.

Account Management:
User accounts are managed by the IT department. Employees should notify IT immediately if they suspect unauthorized account access.
