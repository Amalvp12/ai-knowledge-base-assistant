Employee Handbook - ABC Technologies

Working Hours:
Employees are expected to work from Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

Attendance Policy:
Employees must mark attendance through the company attendance system. Repeated late arrivals may require discussion with the reporting manager.

Leave Policy:
Employees receive:
- 12 Casual Leave days per year
- 12 Sick Leave days per year
- 15 Annual Leave days per year

Work From Home Policy:
Employees may work from home up to two days per week with manager approval.

Dress Code:
Employees are expected to maintain professional attire during office hours and client meetings.

Performance Reviews:
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year to evaluate employee growth and contributions.

Training and Development:
ABC Technologies encourages employees to participate in training programs, workshops, and certification courses.

Employee Benefits:
- Health Insurance
- Paid Annual Leave
- Professional Development Programs
- Performance Bonuses

Code of Conduct:
Employees must maintain professionalism, respect colleagues, and follow company policies.
