Frequently Asked Questions - ABC Technologies

Q: How do I apply for leave?
A: Employees can apply for leave through the HR management portal. Leave requests should be submitted at least one day in advance whenever possible.

Q: How do I contact HR?
A: Employees can contact HR by emailing hr@abcteknologies.com or visiting the HR department during office hours.

Q: What are the office working hours?
A: Office working hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

Q: Is work from home allowed?
A: Yes. Employees may work from home up to two days per week with manager approval.

Q: How do I report technical issues?
A: Technical issues should be reported to the IT support team through the helpdesk portal.

Q: How often are performance reviews conducted?
A: Performance reviews are conducted twice a year.

Q: What benefits are provided to employees?
A: Employees receive health insurance, paid leave, training opportunities, and performance bonuses.

Q: How can I access company training programs?
A: Employees can register through the Learning and Development portal.

Q: What should I do if I forget my company account password?
A: Contact the IT support team to reset your password.

Q: Where are the company offices located?
A: ABC Technologies has offices in Kochi and Bangalore.
